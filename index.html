Welcome!

TrueTag is a smart system designed to help brands prevent product theft and confirm product authenticity. Based on NFC and QR technology, it offers brands and buyers peace of mind.

About TrueTag

Developed by Viktor Bocskor & AI Partner, TrueTag aims to modernize product security and build trust between brands and customers. Our system is discreet, effective, and scalable for various industries.

Technology

Our solution uses a combination of encrypted NFC tags and QR codes to register, track, and verify authenticity of physical products. When scanned, the system checks against the TrueTag database for validity.
